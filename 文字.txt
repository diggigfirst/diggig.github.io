Do you know how to make a steamd vermicelli roll?
Let me tell you.
First,put the flour,lettuce and an egg on the table.
Next,break the egg and mix it up with a bowl.
Third,mix enough flour and water together in another bowl.
Forth,pour oil on a iron plate and put the plate into the pot.
Fifth,put flour mix ,lettuce and egg on the plate and steam it.
Sixth,took out the plate with a tong.
Seventh,roll it with a borad and put it in another plate.
Finlly,pour the soy on it and eat it!
